How to make a Pancit Recipe

Ingredient

  • 1(12 ounce)package dried rice noodles
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable
  • 1 onion,finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic,minced
  • 2 cups diced cooled chicken breast meat
  • 1 small head cabbage,thinly sliced
  • 4 carrot,thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 lemons-cut into wedges,for garnnish

    • Directions

    step 1

    place rice noodles in a large bowl;cover with
    with warm water and let soften for 8 to 10 minutws.Drain and set aside

    step 2

    Meanwhile,heat oil in a work or large skillet over a medirum-low heat.Add onion
    and garlic;cook and stir until onion is tender,about 3 to 5 minutes.stir in
    chicken,cabbage ,carrot,and soy sauce.cook until cabbage begins to soften
    Toss in noodles and cook,stirring constantly,until heated through

    step 3

    Transfer pancit to a serving dish and garnish with lemon wedges.
